Eco and Natural Credentials

Natural

The term Natural is often used to refer to products that have been produced using only unprocessed ingredients and which are free from artificial ingredients and additives. The marketing strategies for natural foods may be understood on two levels.

First: natural foods are consideredunprocessed foods in the sense that they are not influenced by industry for mass production.

Second: natural foods are seen as. Both dimensions are marketed as healthy for people and the environment.

Energy Efficent

Energy Efficiency is the principle by which products are designed to consume as little energy as possible.

Buying energy efficient products is a great way to reduce your carbon footprint and your energy bill.

Vegan

Vegans endeavour not to use or consume animal products of any kind. This means that Vegan products must, as far as is possible and practical, be entirely free from animal involvement.

Vegetable, mineral or plant/mineral-derived synthetic alternatives are used instead. Vegan products are free from animal testing.

Biodegradable

Biodegradable products can be naturally broken down into their basic elements found in nature and absorbed back into the natural environment in a short period of time.

Buying biodegradable products helps reduce waste, as unlike other popular materials today they don't take hundreds or thousands of years to decompose. This means less waste and a cleaner environment.

Recycled

Recycled Product are made in whole or in part from materials recovered from waste.

By buying recycled products you help to reduce the amount of materials going to landfill, which conserves resources and energy and helps ensure demand for reclaimed materials.

Organic

Organic agriculture is a system of growing crops and rearing animals through natural processes and in natural conditions, with minimal artificial stimuli and disruption to the environment.

The use of pesticides, chemical fertilisers, insecticides, fungicides and routine antibiotics are either disallowed completely or they are restricted.

This product has following certificates

USDA Organic certification

The National Organic Program and the Organic Foods Production Act are intended to assure consumers that the organic foods they purchase are produced, processed, and certified to be consistent with national organic standards.

Organic Trust Ltd

The Organic Trust was founded in Ireland in 1991 by a core group of dedicated organic producers including some of the pioneers of organic food production in Ireland. The Organic Trust offers a professional, user-friendly Organic Inspection and Certification Service for a vast range of organic enterprises including the full range of organic food. The scope of our certification covers the most prolific range of organic enterprises in Ireland at the present time. Currently this includes all organic livestock enterprises from organic beef, lamb and poultry units to the more specialised enterprises of organic dairy, pig, goat and deer production.

Fairly Traded

Fairtrade guarantees that disadvantaged producers have been paid a fair price for their goods.

When buying Fairtrade you can be confidant a fair price has been paid for the goods, and a premium beyond this which allows the producers to cover the cost of production and invest money back into their communities in the form of social, economic or environmental projects.

Forest Friendly

Forest Friendly products contain wood from well-managed forests. The management practices of these forests ensure that they are harvested using sustainable principles that ensure the forest is not over exploited, protecting it for the future.

Buying forest friendly products also helps to protect forests from destruction by illegal logging as forest friendly timber has a complete chain of custody, which proves where the timber has come from.

Water Conservation

Water Conservation is based on reducing water consumption and waste.

Water and energy consumption are closely linked, as energy is required to transport and heat water in your home as well as to treat it after it disappears down the plughole. This makes saving water a great way to reduce your energy bills and your carbon footprint.

Animal Friendly

Animal Friendly products are free from animal testing and ensure no animals were killed or harmed during the production and natural habitats wernt disturbed.
