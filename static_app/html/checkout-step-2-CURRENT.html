Need Help with Buying?

  • Buying things on Eco Market is easy peasy.

    Simply follow the steps here in the checkout and remember that if you buy items from more than one stall owner you will need to pay them separately (our checkout will guide you through this).

    You will get an email when your item has been dispatched and you can always contact the stall owner at any time to ask questions about your order by going to your My Account area and messaging the stall owner using our private messaging system.
