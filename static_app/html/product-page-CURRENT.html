Organic Argan Oil Cosmetic Skincare 100ml

by Organic Argan Oil UK

Product of Morocco. 100% Organic Argan Oil. 50m bottle. Ideal for skin, hair and nail care.

Our Pure Argan Oil is 100% pure, natural and certified organic and vegan. There are no additives or preservatives, and it's free from artificial ingredients, synthetic colours and perfumes.

About Argan Oil.

Argan Oil can help you look better and feel healthier, inside and out.
You can use organic cosmetic Argan Oil to soften, protect and nourish your skin, hair and nails. Your skin and hair will feel silky smooth, your nails will be stronger and better looking and you'll notice a reduction in visible signs of ageing.


Argan Oil comes from the kernels of the rare Argan Tree (Argania Spinosa L.), known locally as the 'Tree of Life'.

Argan Oil is a rare and precious oil, used for centuries by the people of Morocco for its naturally healthy properties.

Rich in Vitamin E and high in essential fatty acids, pure and organic Argan Oil is one of the best kept secrets in health and beauty.


There are two main types of natural Argan Oil. Cosmetic Argan Oil is used for beautiful and healthy skin, hair and nails. Argan Food Oil, with its rich and delicious nutty flavour, is one of the healthiest vegetable oils, loaded with essential vitamins and nutrients.


Argan Oil protects and moisturises the skin, combats dry skin, reduces signs of visible ageing and tackles the appearance of wrinkles and damage caused by stress, sun and wind.
Argan Oil is ideal for all skin types, including the most sensitive skins. It has been used as a traditional remedy for a range of skin conditions, including psoriasis and eczema. It is also used to reduce the scars left behind by adolescent acne, other scars and to reduce and prevent the appearance of stretch marks.

Argan Oil adds a beautiful and healthy sheen to hair, leaving it feeling silky soft and smooth.

Pure, natural Argan Oil can be used with lemon juice to repair and strengthen damaged and brittle nails.

Argan Oil can also be used as part of your shaving routine, moisturising and nourishing the skin and helping to prevent shaving rash, itchiness, cuts and shaving nicks.

Argan Oil has also been traditionally used as an anti-inflammatory for conditions such as arthritis and rheumatism.
