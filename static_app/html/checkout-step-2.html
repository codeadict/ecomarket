How Checkout Works

  • Is the marketplace global?

    Eco Market recently launched internationally due to huge demand from shoppers and sellers outside the UK. The shipping system enables you to easily set up your Stall's shipping profiles so that you can offer different shipping rates for different countries or regions. If your not yet ready to go global, dont worry you can select to ship to only shoppers in your country.

  • How our fees work.

    It’s free to create a Stall and list unlimited products for sale on Eco Market. When a product sells we charge a 10% sales commission on the total order value including the postage costs.

  • Is it free to close my Stall?

    Yes, we never charge you to close your Stall on Eco Market and the is no minimum contract length. If you dont like selling on the site you can easily close you stall anytime, however we are sure you will love your time here!

  • How do I list a product with product options?

    We suggest you list the different options that are available in the product description as a bullet points list and include photos of the different colours as images in your listing, you can list up to 10 images per product. In the product description request that the shopper leaves you a note in the special instructions box as to which option they would like during checkout.
