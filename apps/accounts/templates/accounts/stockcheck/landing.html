Doing a stock check is fast & easy!

Once you have logged into Eco Market doing a stock check is an easy but important part of selling your products here. The products you have published on Eco Market must always be available for sale which keeps customers happy since they will keep coming back! You simply need to check all the inventory amounts are correct on the products you have for sale right now and confirm this.

Common questions you might be wondering about.

  • Why do I need to start doing a stock check on Eco Market?

    Stock checks were introduced to ensure that when a customer buys an item, their order can be fulfilled. Since our customers are your customers, keeping them happy is within everyones interest!

  • How often do I need to do these stock checks?

    Your first stock check must be completed by November 20th 2013, after then you must do a stock check at least once every 30 days. This is to ensure that the stock you have live on Eco Market is accurate.

  • Who is Eco Market? Why are my products for sale here?

    Eco Market is a an eco-friendly marketplace selling thousands of products from thousands of sellers across the world. If you have reached this page, its very likely either you (or someone within your business) created a 'stall' to sell products here (if it helps, we used to be called Ethical Community some time ago!)

  • How do I do a stock check? Can I get more help?

    It's extremely easy. You sign in using the form above, or if you want to watch a screencast video taking you through the entire process you can click the link above to visit our help centre.
